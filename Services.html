What makes QFO unique?

Quick fix orthodontics uses a technique new to the orthodontics world. As soon as the teeth that are being corrected have finished moving, we imediatly tighten the braces again. In conventional orthodontics, the teeth are allowed to set before moving them. However, this takes far longer than at quick fix orthodontics. Even the most damaged smiles can be repaired within two months! So come to QFO to be welcomed with warm smiles and you'll be leaving with a warm smile of your own before you know it.

Why you should join the QFO family: Here at QFO, we are a tight comunity. We have team bonding events every friday for our employees and customers! Not to mention, our fast fixing technology is not only faster but cheaper than traditional orthodontistry.
